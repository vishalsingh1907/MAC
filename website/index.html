Who We Are

Empowering Students Through Technology

The Microsoft Ambassadors Club at Lingaya's Vidyapeeth is an official chapter under the Centre of Excellence. We are a growing and passionate community focused on learning by doing.

We host workshops, sessions, and activities that help students gain practical experience with Microsoft tools, cloud computing, and modern collaboration — right here on campus.
